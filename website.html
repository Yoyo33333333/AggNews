our services

News Articles

NewsFuse extracts articles based on user preferences and criteria.

Categorized Articles

Articles are categorized into predefined topics such as politics, sports, technology, and entertainment.

Article Metadata

Metadata including headlines, summaries, publication dates, and sources are extracted and presented to users.

Notification

Users receive notifications when new articles matching their preferences are available.

Geolocation

NewsFuse can extract articles or information based on their geolocation, such as news from a specific city or country

Trending Topics

NewsFuse can provide insights into trending topics or keywords based on extracted article data.
